About Us

Springster celebrates the diverse, inspirational and convention-defying experiences of millions of girls globally, propelling them to take on their world.

Our Story

Springster was developed specifically to champion vulnerable girls after research across our four flagship markets showed that girls critical lack of knowledge and confidence prevented them from identifying and reaching goals in their life.

Girls benefit from reassurance and advice generated by shared stories and experiences from other girls like them. As a result they are enabled to provide support and guidance for each other. Springster provides a platform to share specially curated content, services, tips and advice that make a tangible impact in their lives, from overcoming everyday problems like how to save money, or start a difficult conversation with their parents - through to giving them confidence and becoming less isolated.

How we do that

Springster is a global mobile-first platform which builds vulnerable girls’ confidence and skills online to help them fulfill their potential offline.

We believe girls find meaning and strength in each other’s experiences. Through entertaining and educational content and stories written for and by girls we can create positive change in girls’ lives by building their knowledge, resilience and self-belief.

By tracking real time usage, comments and user engagement, Springster uses a new impact methodology to measure how online behaviour can shape offline action and lead to shifts in girls’ knowledge, attitude and behaviour.
